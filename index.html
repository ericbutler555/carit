Welcome to the Center.

Welcome to the Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT).

CARIT is an interdisciplinary research center that provides high quality survey research, program evaluation, performance measurement, and experimental research using scientifically rigorous methods. CARIT is a valuable resource for technologists in need of assistance for research projects, grants, and contracts. In addition, community organizations and agencies can utilize CARIT in a variety of areas such as program evaluation or technology surveys. CARIT will provide an environment that focuses on facilitating an applied learning environment for KSU graduate and undergraduate students with hands-on research experience in the areas of data collection, data management and analysis.

We hope that you will peruse the website to learn more about our CARIT faculty. You can also find information about upcoming events scheduled at our main CARIT campus or any of our satellite centers.
